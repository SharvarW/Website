MULTIPHYSICS

Chip Package System Co-design

We live in a world where multiple physical forces are at play, and the products we produce are often subject to many of these physical forces simultaneously. Fluid forces, thermal effects, structural integrity and electromagnetic radiation can all impact performance of products and industrial processes. If you try to isolate the multiple forces in play, you may not get an accurate prediction of behavior. AiCAD multiphysics solutions can help engineers examine these effects in combination and isolation, achieving the highest fidelity solution when it's needed.
